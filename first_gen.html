First-Gen Wolverines!

Welcome, First-Gen Students!

Around 4,000 students at the University of Michigan are the first in their families to attend college. Although being the first to achieve something can feel overwhelming, embarking on a college journey is also a significant life milestone.

Fortunately, U-M offers valuable networks and programs designed to help you connect and succeed on campus. Use this website to discover the resources available to you. Explore your opportunities, find your community, and make you feel at home!

Student Stories

Sharing your experiences with others is a powerful way to build connections. Student stories highlight the challenges and triumphs of navigating university life. These stories foster a sense of belonging and inspire others. You can also submit your own story to contribute to this collective experience and empower fellow students.

Get Involved!

Getting involved on campus is a great way to meet new people, develop new skills, and make the most of your university experience. Whether through clubs, organizations, or campus events, there are endless opportunities to engage with the university community and grow personally and academically.
